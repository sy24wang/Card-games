A Game will have 2 player, computer(A.I.) and human player

Rule are as follows:

Card values:

J = 10
Q = 10
K = 10
A = 11 or 1
all other cards are of their own face value

if hit 21, game ends immediately

otherwise, A.I. may catch up with player



player wins when:

1 - Player's score == 21  (game ends immediately)
2 - AI's did not manage to catch up (AI score < Player score)
3 - AI's score > 21

player loses when:

1 - Player's score > 21
2 - AI managed to catch up with player (AI score == Player score)


others:

split (2 of the same card, ex. 4,4 or J, J)
double wager
